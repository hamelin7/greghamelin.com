Experienced Technician and Relationship Manager

I.T. Technician - Client Support

Greg began his IT career at Paragus as a technician, honing his skills before graduating from the IT program at STCC. His dedication and expertise led him to working closely with MBK, handling over 90% of their technical cases, establishing himself as a trusted and highly capable professional.

Account Relationship Manager

Greg transitioned from his hands-on technical role, where he worked closely with a diverse range of clients, to become one of Paragus’ first Account Relationship Managers. Committed to continuous growth, he further expanded his expertise by pursuing a dual major in IT and Computer Science at Elms College.

Board of Directors

Since joining the board in 2022, Greg has served as a member of the Governance, Compensation, and Nomination Committee. In this role, he has played a key part in interviewing and hiring new board members, as well as helping shape the board’s compensation strategy.
